FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know about working with Yanmu Studio

How long does a typical pitch deck project take?
Most pitch deck projects take 2-3 weeks from discovery to final delivery. This includes strategy calls, multiple revision rounds, and unlimited edits until you're 100% satisfied. Rush delivery (1 week) is available for POESAM/competition winners at no extra cost.
Do you work with pre-seed startups with limited budgets?
Yes! We offer split payment plans (40% upfront, 30% at draft, 30% at delivery) and can accept equity (0.1-0.25%) for exceptional pre-seed startups. We also have special pricing for prize winners and can work with local currencies (TND, NGN, GHS).
What makes you different from other design agencies?
We're not just designers — we're hardware founders. With 2 years building IoT devices, 250+ pitches reviewed, and 15+ hackathons won (including helping Ecofeed win GreenTIC 2024), we understand the technical complexity AND the investor mindset. We've been in your shoes.
Can you help with non-English presentations?
Absolutely. We offer bilingual (EN/FR) materials as standard, and can add Arabic for international MENA competitions. All our decks are optimized for both European and African investors, with cultural nuances built in.
What if I need changes after the final delivery?
We include 30 days of post-delivery support for minor tweaks and updates. For ongoing needs, we offer monthly retainer packages ($500-1,000/month) for continuous design support as your startup evolves.
Do you offer white-label services for agencies/accelerators?
Yes! We partner with accelerators, VCs, and consulting firms to provide white-label design services for their portfolio companies. We work behind the scenes while you maintain the client relationship. Contact us for partnership pricing.
